# ECON 481A — Data Science for Economists — University of Washington (2026)

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How is grading distributed in this course?** The course grade is based entirely on your [final project](https://eisenhauerio.github.io/courses-business-decisions/course-projects/index.html). This course is an opportunity to explore the exciting space of data science - it's up to you to make the most of it through your project work throughout the quarter. I am always available to point you towards exciting areas to explore and will continuously share material in our [Slack community](https://join.slack.com/t/eisenhauerioworkspace/shared_invite/zt-3lxtc370j-XLdokfTkno54wfhHVxvEfA).

**When is the project due?** The project is due at 11:59 PM PDT on March 20th. You will lose access to your student repo immediately after the deadline.
